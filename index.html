Douglas Siqueira is a recent graduate in UX UI Design from the University of Toronto, with a background in Web and Graphic Design.




 

BIO

UX designer, with a background in graphic and web design, trained at the University of Toronto UX/UI boot camp.
Skilled in Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and user-centric design research with a proficiency in delivering user experiences across desktop, mobile, tablet and other channels. As a graphic/web designer, led a team to successfully meet branding and technical requirements for various projects including websites/landing pages, logos, email campaigns, and social media content creation. Strengths lie in defining and executing design strategy built on a deep understanding of both user and business needs. Specializing in user research, usability testing, information architecture, and interaction design, I aim to create interfaces that bring users joy and increase brand loyalty.
